Who We Are

Learn about our team

Our mission is to feed and educate undergraduate students with a cheaper and healthier alternative to any campus meal plan. To accomplish our goal, we deliver fresh ingredients with simple, easy-to-follow recipes so students can learn to cook in their own kitchens without too much time and great hassle. We have assembled a diverse team to carry out our mission, all Carnegie Mellon students with various expertise. Read about them below!
